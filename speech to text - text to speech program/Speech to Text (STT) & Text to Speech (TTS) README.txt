Speech to Text (STT) is an technology that helps you convert your speech to text. 

It can recognize a wide variety of languages and related dialects. 
In order to work with this extension, simply open the addon's UI and then press on the big microphone icon to start converting your voice to text. Please note that, when the add-on is first started, it asks for microphone permission. Please accept the permission to start working with the add-on. 

Text to speech (TTS) is a technology that converts text into spoken audio. 
It can read aloud PDFs, websites, and books using natural AI voices.

Speech recognition is the process of converting audio into text. This is commonly used in voice assistants like Alexa, Siri, etc.
